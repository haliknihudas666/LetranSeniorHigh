How to Apply

STEP 1: Online Application

1. Fill-out the application form online at www.letran.edu.ph/admission. Available entrance examination dates are posted online. You may choose your desired schedule.
2. Once you have accomplished the form, you will be given an application number. Please keep the application number for fast and easy transaction. NOTE:
3. All Senior High and Collegiate Transferees are required to go through assessment of grades and interview with the College Dean prior taking the entrance examination thus, students applying for the said levels should skip the selection of examination date.
4. For assessment of grades and interview, you may visit the Letran Admissions Office, Mondays to Fridays, 9:00 A.M. to 4:00 P.M. Please be reminded to bring your Transcript of Records (TOR) or your certified true copy of grades on the day of your visit, as no one will not be allowed to proceed to the interview without the said requirements.

STEP 2: Submission of Requirements

1. Submit the required documents for application to the Letran Admissions Office at least two (2) working days before the scheduled entrance examination. The office is open Mondays to Fridays, 8:00 A.M. to 5:00 P.M. to accommodate submissions and applications.
2. Please take note that the required documents should be placed inside a long brown envelope.
3. See list of admission requirements here.

STEP 3: Payment of Entrance Examination Fee

1. Entrance Examination fee is Php 500.00. The said fee shall be settled at the school Cashier only after the application requirements has been submitted.
2. For more details or inquiries, you may call the Letran Admissions Office at 527-7693 loc. 345 or 641. Want to know more about scholarships? Click here.
3. Follow us on our social media accounts! (Links to Facebook & Twitter)

Requirements

FOR NEW STUDENTS

• Two (2) 2” x 2” recent picture with white background
• Certified true copies of the student applicant’s complete 9th Grade report card and the latest 10th Grade report card (at least up to 1st grading period).
• Photocopy of Latest School I.D. card
• Photocopy of NSO Birth Certificate
• Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate (For Catholic applicants only)
• Photocopy of Alien Certificate of Registration (For foreign applicants only)

FOR TRANSFREES

• Two (2) 2” x 2” recent picture with white background
• Certified true copy of the latest report card. (At least up to 2nd grading period)
• Photocopy of Latest School I.D. card
• Photocopy of NSO Birth Certificate
• Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate (For Catholic applicants only)
• Photocopy of Alien Certificate of Registration (For foreign applicants only)
